Goodwill Lounge RESTAURANT & RESIDENCE 38 VICTORIA STREET — DURBAN Telephone 22780 “EXCELLENT CUISINE” THE SOUTH AFRICAN INDIAN WHO'S WHO PREFACE THE third edition of the South African Indian Who’s Who is now presented to the public. Since its inception and the publication of the first edition it has fulfilled the purposes for which it was intended, namely, to provide a documentary record of the achievements of Indians in the Union of South Africa and of those engaged in trade, commerce and other professions. The demand for such a publication has not been confined to Indians only but also includes Europeans who have commercial and other business contacts with the Business Houses of the Indian Community. Further, its circulation reaches far beyond the limits of the Union. A large number of copies of this publication goes outside the Union of South Africa, thus affording a ready reference in ascertaining information about public, commercial and professional personalities. This issue will show what great strides the Indians within the Union have made in all matters connected with religious, educational, social and sports matters in the life of the Indian community. Compared with other Communities elsewhere this progress and evidence of self-expression and development and advancement in a relatively short period, despite the many handicaps and hardships they have had to endure, place them second to none in comparison with any other Com- munity similarly placed elsewhere. Mr. Dhanee Bramdaw who began this Issue of the Who’s Who suddenly met a tragic end on the 4th July 1952, while on a voyage to England. He was then compiling this third edition, but his end, regretted by a large circle of friends and the public, interrupted its publication for some time. This Issue is dedicated to his memory for his devoted and self-sacrificing labours in the interests of his people. I desire to place on record my sincere thanks to all who assisted me in the compilation of this Edition. Their unstinted support and help have enabled me in a great measure to produce this publication. Bramdaw Publications, Pietermaritzburg, Cc ‘ \ 2 man, hh 4 Natal, S.A.
